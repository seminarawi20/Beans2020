Please read this carefully before clicking accept.

This is an academic research study on decision making under uncertainty of a tipping point. You will be matched with co- players and you will be asked to make a decision after answering two survey questions for your general understanding.

Duration and reward:
The entire game will take 7-10 minutes. Your payment consists of a fixed reward of $0.50 for successful completion and a bonus of $0.20 for every point you earn. The points you get depend on your decision, the decision of your co-players as well as on chance.

Confidentiality:
All data we collect is treated confidentially and will only be used for our research purpose. Your name will not be linked to the results in any way.

Requirements:
To participate, you need to be located in the United States of America. You may not have participated in this study before. There are no other formal requirements for participation.

Voluntary participation:
Participation in this study is voluntary. If you do not want to take part in the study, please do not accept. If you discontinue participation during the game, you will not receive any bonus.
