Thank you for participating.
If you read these rules carefully and choose wisely, you can earn a bonus up to US$ 2.50 by participating in this game.
Completing this task will take about 7 minutes and it is important that you pay close attention during this time so that you do not spoil the task for you and the other participants.
After you are given the instructions, there comes the task. The task has three stages.
First, you take a small quiz about the game. It consists of two questions.
Then you are matched with two other participants. Here you are asked to make a real decision.
Finally, you are asked to fill out a short survey.
Note that only the decision part will affect your payment.
